ApplyPreset - As the name suggests. (Takes a string input, searches for a preset with that name, and applies it to the currently hovered clip.)

QuickSplitAll - Splits all clips currently under the playhead. (Finds playhead and uses the razor tool with shift held down to split all clips at that position)

QuickSplitHere - Splits the clip currently under the mouse. (Switches to razor tool, clicks, switches back)
